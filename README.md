This web is a place to help victims of Deepfake Scams or Terrors in Indonesia. 
The target is to help victims find justice and help guide them on how to report their case
The web also showcases stories from other victims to help them feel like they are not alone
This also helps them to be more careful in the future from the insights they may receive.
Or they themselves can share their story, they have a choice as well, whether or not they want to stay anonymous when sharing.

Files are divided from frontend and backend
backend uses Supabase.
